Why Humber?

Because

The most important for me is...

Specialty

I am amazed at the number of specialties offered, their diversity and the social life of Humber. It was in "Humber" that I managed to find the specialty that I had been looking for so long, or rather, "Multimedia Design and Development". Someone may ask why “Humber”, when other colleges also have this specialty? The main reason I chose is a detailed description of the classes of each semester, which ideally fit the criteria I have expected.  Photoshop, Final Cut Pro; web technologies such as HTML5, Cascading Style Sheets (CSS3), JavaScript, jQuery. This is exactly the development environments that I would like to work in!

Work after graduation

After reading a lot of information about the Humber, I found out that 83% of students who graduated from your college find work within 6 months, which is a very high rate! For me personally, this means that you provide very high-quality knowledge because of which the employer is ready to accept prospective employees in his company. This is important for me, because in the future I want to have a good career growth!

Practice

I am very attracted to the fact that "Humber" provides work practice already during the educational process. I believe that this will help me develop my skills at a higher level and prepare for the beginning of the work process during the last semester. Practice in computer programming is one of the main components of becoming a good specialist, which is why I would like to study at Humber!

Reviews

As a rule, we all draw our conclusions about something based on feedback and recommendations. Before submitting documents to "Humber" I carefully studied the reviews about this institution and made sure that my choice was correct. Most of the real Humber students talk about the high professionalism of your teachers, the high quality of the knowledge provided, the friendly atmosphere and the wonderful working atmosphere in your institution!

Still why Humber?

In general, I would like to say that Humber really attracted me with the quality of the education offered, the reviews from former and current students, the practice provided, it’s active social life, and the friendly atmosphere. Study in your College would be a significant step in my life, after which I could proudly say that I became a "Humber" graduate and prove myself as a real specialist.
